Welcome to
Quirky Cafés Aarhus!

Do you like going to cafés but have some trouble finding the ones that have more life and personality to them than the average Starbucks?
If the answer is yes, you are at the right place, as on this website we will be sharing some cute and unique cafés that are not just about the coffee.
The purpose of the website is to share the knowledge that probably only the locals would know with the people visiting Aarhus,
or just moving in; this way we can all get a more authentic experience with the Danes.
