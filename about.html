About

The idea behind Quirky Cafés Aarhus is to share all those hidden little cafés that offer more of experience as well.
The cafés mentioned on the website all give something unique, something to do with friends, or in a special environment.
We made this website in hopes of helping all internationals in Aarhus, whether on vacation or on any other business,
to find things to do in the beautiful city of Aarhus, after visiting the big sights the city has to offer.

Write Your Suggestions:
