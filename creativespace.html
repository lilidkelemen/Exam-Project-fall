Creative Space

- the café where you can design your own mug

About the Café

Creative Space is a pottery café, where you can enjoy spending time creatively while also getting a cup of coffee out of it.
The café also exists in Copenhagen, and is incredibly popular, especially for the locals.
The atmosphere in the cafés is immensely cozy, and you can spend your time painting here. It is an ideal spot for get together with your friends or family,
where it is most certain that no one will be looking at their phones.
Creative Space offers you a great variety of mugs, cups, plates and bowls to paint, with the materials, painting, and glazing included in the price.
It is also an ideal spot to with kids, because they get to be as creative as they want.
